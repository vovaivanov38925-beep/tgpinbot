# 🎬 Pinterest to Action — Рекламное видео 30 секунд

## Полный промт для генерации видео (на английском для AI):

```
Create a 30-second vertical mobile app advertisement video (9:16 aspect ratio) for "Pinterest to Action" - a Telegram Mini App that helps women turn their Pinterest saves into real actions.

STYLE: Feminine, aesthetic, dreamy. Pink, lavender, peach gradient color palette. Soft magical atmosphere with sparkles and hearts. Smooth cinematic motion.

---

SCENE BREAKDOWN:

SCENE 1 (0-8 seconds) - PROBLEM:
- Young woman (25-30 years old, natural beauty) sits on a beige couch in an empty white room
- She looks frustrated, overwhelmed at her smartphone
- Phone shows Pinterest app with hundreds of saved pins
- Soft natural lighting with slight blue/grey tint
- Text overlay: "100 сохранённых... 0 в реальности?" (in Russian)

SCENE 2 (8-15 seconds) - SOLUTION:
- Close-up of smartphone screen
- Beautiful pink-lavender app interface opens
- Shows: user avatar, "Уровень 1" (Level 1), "⚡ 0" points
- Gallery grid of cards with icons: 🍳 (recipe), 👗 (fashion), 🏠 (home), 🧘‍♀️ (fitness)
- Sparkles and magical particles around the phone
- Text overlay: "Pinterest to Action" (app name)

SCENE 3 (15-22 seconds) - FEATURES:
- User copies Pinterest link (show chocolate cake image)
- Pastes into app
- AI animation: scanning effect → category badge "🍳 Рецепты" appears
- Points animation: "✨ +10 очков!"
- Create task screen with date picker: "Напомнить: Воскресенье, 18:00"
- Bell icon 🔔 with notification animation

SCENE 4 (22-30 seconds) - RESULT:
- Split screen transition:
  * BEFORE: sad woman, empty room (left side, desaturated)
  * AFTER: happy smiling woman, beautifully decorated pink room (right side, warm colors)
- Phone shows: "Уровень 5", "350 очков", "12 выполнено"
- Achievement badges unlock animation: "🏆 Первый шаг" "🏆 Начинающий"
- Final text: "Хватит сохранять — начинай делать!"
- "Открыть в Telegram" button

---

TECHNICAL SPECS:
- Duration: 30 seconds
- Format: Vertical 9:16 (720x1280 or 1080x1920)
- FPS: 30
- Style: Modern feminine aesthetic, soft gradients, smooth animations
- Music: Inspiring instrumental (no lyrics), upbeat but gentle
- All UI text in Russian language

COLOR PALETTE:
- Primary: Pink gradient (#FFB6C1 → #DDA0DD)
- Accent: Lavender (#E6E6FA)
- Background: Peach/Cream (#FFF5EE)
- Text: White on gradients, dark gray on light backgrounds

EMOTIONAL JOURNEY:
- Scene 1: Frustration, overwhelm (sad/grey tones)
- Scene 2: Hope, discovery (pink sparkles appear)
- Scene 3: Engagement, excitement (bright animations)
- Scene 4: Joy, accomplishment (warm golden tones)
```
